# MathLensAI
MathLensAI, the best interactive math journal that can visualize, reason, and teach as a never exhausted tutor of the highest caliber and then some.
1. One-Tap Magic (free tier)

Direct your camera → solution provided in 0.8 s
Cursive, print, or even blackboard - it works without any problem
A step-by-step guide laid out on your photo so that you can see precisely where every line is located
50 free daily solves, ads-free, and no need for an account

2. Lens Pro - the subscription that changes “answer” into “mastery”
$4.99 / month (first 7 days free)

Unlimited solves plus 4K photo zoom
“Why?” button: tap any step → 15-sec voice explanation + animated replay
“Hard mode”: final answer is hidden until you input your own, then you get scored like a coach
“Fix my mistake”: circle your incorrect line → AI rewrites only that part and clarifies the gap
3D graphs that rotate, zoom, and allow you to move variables with your finger
Night-mode whiteboard: solving on infinite digital paper, exporting as PDF with your handwriting being preserved

3. Your Personal Math Wingman - “Leo”

Always-on chat bubble for every problem
Speaks 27 languages, identifies your mood (“I’m stuck!”) and shifts to micro-hints
Learns your weak spots: spent 3 days on quadratics? Leo arranges 2-minute daily drills in your camera roll
Group mode: share a live session, Leo teaches 4 friends simultaneously, color-coding every person’s steps

4. Teacher Portal (free for verified educators)

Auto-grade a picture of 30 paper tests in 20 seconds
Class mistakes heat-map → 1-click mini-lesson generator
Exportable report cards with “Leo’s growth tips” for every kid

5. Under the hood - why it’s smarter

2026-vintage multi-modal AI trained on 2 billion real student mistakes (not just correct answers)
On-device model for instant first step, cloud burst for PhD-level proofs
